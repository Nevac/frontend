WTH IS RETIMED ??

Retimed is a local multiplayer arena shooter for 2 to 4 players. When a player is in danger, time slows down in a bubble around them. This gives players enough time to react and try to dodge the deadly projectiles. These time bubbles generate exciting alternations between fast-paced movements and tension-filled, slow motion moments.
